General Inquiries

Can I tour the studio before joining?

Absolutely. We host free public studio tours every first Friday of the month from 5 PM to 7 PM. You can also contact us to schedule a private walkthrough.

Do you offer gift cards for workshops?

Yes, digital and physical gift cards are available for any denomination. They can be applied to workshops, memberships, or fine art print purchases.

Where can I park?

There is free street parking available along Artisan Way, and a paid parking garage located just two blocks north on 4th Street.
