Pricing FAQs

Questions about billing and memberships.

Are there any hidden fees with memberships?

No hidden fees. Membership fees cover access to the space and heavy equipment. Consumables like paper and specific colored inks must be purchased separately.

Do you offer student or artist discounts?

Yes, we offer a 15% discount on memberships for currently enrolled art students and recent graduates (within 1 year). Please contact us with your ID.

What is the cancellation policy for workshops?

Full refunds are provided for cancellations made at least 7 days before the workshop start date. Cancellations within 7 days will receive studio credit.
