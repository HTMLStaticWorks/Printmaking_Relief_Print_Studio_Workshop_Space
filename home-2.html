The Workshop Experience

How our printmaking sessions work.

01

Design

Draft and transfer your original sketch onto the printing block with precision.

02

Use professional gouges to carefully carve away the negative space, bringing your design to life.

03

Ink

04

Print

Run your block through our vintage etching presses for the final dramatic reveal.
