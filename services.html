Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our services.

Do I need prior experience for workshops?

Not at all! Our introductory classes are designed specifically for beginners with absolutely no prior printmaking experience.

Are materials included in the workshop fee?

Yes, all basic materials (inks, blocks, standard paper, carving tools) are provided. You may bring special paper if you wish.

What does a studio membership include?

Memberships include access to our etching presses, screen exposure units, drying racks, and washout booths. You must provide your own consumables like paper and personal inks.
